# AnswerCare AI — Competitor Analysis

## Direct AI Competitors (Name These Confidently)

### Ringwell
- Price: $49–99/mo
- Positioning: AI call routing and answering for small businesses
- What they do well: Low price point, self-serve setup, basic call routing
- Where they fall short (for trades): No calendar booking integration, no trade-specific scripts, no job qualification flow, no SMS dispatch to tech + customer
- Our angle: "Ringwell routes calls. AnswerCare books jobs."
- Landing page: /compare/ringwell-alternative

### Voios
- Price: Varies / $49–129/mo range
- Positioning: AI receptionist for small businesses
- What they do well: Multi-industry support, basic scheduling
- Where they fall short: Generic, not trade-specific, no emergency routing logic
- Our angle: Built specifically for trades — knows the difference between "my pipe is leaking" and "my pipe burst, water everywhere"

### Capta
- Price: ~$79–149/mo
- Positioning: AI call agent for service businesses
- What they do well: Lead capture, CRM integration
- Where they fall short: Heavy CRM focus, overly complex for one-truck operator
- Our angle: "No CRM required. Your calendar + SMS is enough."

## Traditional Answering Services

### Ruby Receptionists / AnswerConnect / PATLive
- Price: $150–300/mo (message taking) to $500+/mo (scheduling)
- What they are: Human-staffed answering services
- Key weakness: Can't book jobs in real time, callbacks lose the customer, no 24/7 at flat rate
- Our angle: "They leave a message. We close the booking."
- Landing page: /compare/answering-service
