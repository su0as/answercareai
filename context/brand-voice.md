# AnswerCare AI — Brand Voice

## Who We Are
AnswerCare AI is an AI phone agent for one-truck trade operators. We answer calls, qualify customers, book jobs, and text the tech the details — all in under 2 seconds, 24/7.

## Target Customer
Solo trade operators: plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, locksmiths, roofers, pest control operators. Typically 1–5 employees. $100K–$600K annual revenue. Too busy to answer every call but can't afford a receptionist.

## Core Differentiator
We book jobs — not just answer calls. A traditional answering service takes a message. AnswerCare closes the booking.

## Voice: How We Sound
- **Direct.** No fluff. No "we're passionate about helping small businesses." Say what it does.
- **Operator-aware.** We know what it's like to be under a sink when a phone rings. We write for people who work with their hands.
- **Honest.** We say $497 is not refundable. We say what the product actually does. Trust comes from telling the truth.
- **Confident without hype.** No "revolutionary AI" or "game-changing technology." Say: "answers in 2 seconds."
- **Concrete.** Dollar amounts, time to value, specific scenarios. "Booked 4 jobs I would have missed" not "increased revenue."

## Voice: What to Avoid
- Corporate speak ("leverage," "synergy," "ecosystem")
- Vague claims ("best-in-class," "industry-leading")
- Technology hype ("cutting-edge AI," "powerful platform")
- Passive voice
- Hedging ("may help," "could potentially")

## Tone By Section
- **Hero:** Punchy. One clear question or sharp claim. No decoration.
- **Story scenes:** Empathetic but unsentimental. The situation is real, the loss is specific.
- **Pain points:** Honest problem framing. The reader should nod.
- **How it works:** Simple. No jargon. Numbered steps, plain English.
- **Pricing:** Transparent. Include the hard stuff (setup fee, non-refundable).
- **FAQ:** Exactly what the customer is actually wondering. Answer the real question.
