## Linking Rules

**From trade pages → other trade pages:**
Each trade page links to 3 related trades in the "Also Built For" section at the bottom.
- Plumbers → HVAC, Electricians, Locksmiths
- HVAC → Plumbers, Electricians, Roofers
- Electricians → Plumbers, HVAC, Locksmiths
- Locksmiths → Plumbers, Electricians, Pest Control
- Roofers → HVAC, Plumbers, Pest Control
- Pest Control → Plumbers, Locksmiths, Roofers

**From trade pages → comparison pages:**
Each trade page should mention and link to the comparison pages once:
- "See how AnswerCare compares to a traditional answering service →"

**From comparison pages → trade pages:**
Both comparison pages link to all 6 trade pages in a chip grid at the bottom.

**From homepage → trade pages:**
Homepage should have a "We work with" or "Built for your trade" section linking to all vertical pages.

**Blog posts → product pages:**
Every blog post ends with a CTA that links to the most relevant trade page + homepage.
